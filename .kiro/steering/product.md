# Product Overview

This is an academic research group website for the SHUD (Simulator of Hydrologic Unstructured Domains) research group. The site serves as:

- An online CV and portfolio for researchers
- A showcase for academic publications and research projects
- A platform for course materials and educational content
- A hub for the SHUD hydrological modeling system documentation

The website is bilingual (English and Chinese) and targets researchers, students, and collaborators in hydrology and earth sciences.

## Key Features

- Bilingual content (English/Chinese) with separate content directories
- Academic publications management with BibTeX support
- Course and educational materials hosting
- Team member profiles and project showcases
- Integration with external resources (Global Hydro Cloud, SHUD Gallery)
- Static book hosting for multiple technical books (SHUD Model, R in Geoscience, Lab Manual, Numerical Methods)
